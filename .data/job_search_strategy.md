## 5. Application Strategy — Platform by Platform

### LinkedIn (HIGHEST PRIORITY)

1. **Set your headline** to: `Infrastructure & DevOps | Proxmox, Docker, Linux | CS Student at PUCPR | Seeking SRE/DevOps Internship`
2. **Create 6 job alerts** with these exact searches:
   - `estágio DevOps Curitiba`
   - `estágio infraestrutura TI Curitiba`
   - `junior DevOps SRE Brazil remote`
   - `analista infraestrutura junior Curitiba`
   - `junior site reliability engineer remote`
   - `intern infrastructure engineer`
3. **Filters:** Set "Date Posted" → Past Week, "Experience Level" → Internship + Entry Level
4. **Set "Open to Work"** with preferred titles: DevOps Engineer, SRE, Systems Administrator, Infrastructure Engineer, Platform Engineer
5. **Post about your homelab** — 1-2 posts/week showing your Proxmox dashboard, Grafana panels, etc. Tag #DevOps #SRE #Homelab #Linux

### Gupy

1. Search each company individually:
   - `grupopositivo.gupy.io` → Search "DevOps" and "infraestrutura"
   - `stefanini.gupy.io` → Search "DevOps" and "estágio TI"
   - `ebanx.gupy.io` or check `boards.greenhouse.io/ebanx`
2. **Create alerts** on each company portal you register with
3. Check weekly: Boticário, Volvo, Renault, Electrolux, Sicredi all use Gupy

### Catho

1. Search: `DevOps estágio Curitiba`, `infraestrutura TI junior Curitiba`, `SRE Curitiba`
2. **Filters:** Curitiba + 50km radius, Estágio + Júnior levels
3. Enable email alerts for "DevOps Curitiba" and "infraestrutura TI Curitiba"

### Indeed BR

1. Search: `DevOps estágio Curitiba`, `infraestrutura junior Curitiba`, `SRE junior remoto`
2. **Filters:** Date → Last 7 days, Experience → Entry Level
3. Create alerts for each search

### RemoteOK / Himalayas / We Work Remotely
